Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Owner Should Know

Small business owners can significantly reduce their tax burden through various deductions. Key areas include home office expenses like mortgage interest and utilities, vehicle and travel costs such as business mileage and accommodations, and professional services including legal and accounting fees. Technology investments, from computers to software subscriptions, are generally deductible, as are marketing and advertising expenses. Proper documentation is crucial - maintain detailed receipts, mileage logs, and financial statements. Working with a tax professional is recommended to maximize deductions while ensuring compliance with ever-changing tax regulations. Understanding these deductions is essential for optimal business financial management.
